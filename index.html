January '23 Series: Tamiya Racing

About this Series

Build your race car and complete in races in a variety of categories on a variety of tracks. To learn more about the cars, visit here. These cars are a great way to get hands on experience on a wide array of topics such as center of gravity, friction, induction, and more.
